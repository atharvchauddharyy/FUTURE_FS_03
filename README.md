🎯 PROJECT BRIEF & PROPOSAL EXPLANATION

• Who the Business Is:
Iron Paradise GYM is a prominent local fitness center based in Ghaziabad, specializing in strength training, cardio conditioning, and customized personal training programs.

• What Problem the Website Solves:
The gym previously had zero online presence, making it invisible to digital users. Potential clients had to physically visit the premises just to check membership plans, operational hours, and facilities, which led to high lead drop-offs. This website bridges that gap by establishing a centralized digital storefront.

• How It Helps Attract Customers:
Operating as a 24/7 digital reception, the website builds instant trust through clean facility galleries and transparent pricing tiers. By embedding clear call-to-action (CTA) buttons, it allows local residents to submit direct inquiries or connect via WhatsApp instantly, significantly converting online search traffic into real-world gym memberships.
